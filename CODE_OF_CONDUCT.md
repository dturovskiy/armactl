# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want `armactl` to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming project for
operators, contributors, and learners.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but be honest about bugs and risks.
- Prefer clear technical feedback over sarcasm or gatekeeping.
- Focus discussions on improving the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Dismissing contributors for asking basic questions.
- Intentionally disruptive or hostile conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block
participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Serious issues should be reported privately to the maintainer.
